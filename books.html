The Braza Story

Born from the vibrant streets of São Paulo and Rio, Braza Restaurant brings the authentic Brazilian churrasco experience to your table. Our gaúcho-style grilling tradition honors the fire, the salt, and the craft of centuries-old barbecue.

Every cut is hand-selected by our 'Mestre Churrasqueiro' and roasted over charcoal embers. At Braza, we don’t just serve meals — we create memories around the fire. Samba, friendship, and the sizzle of picanha await you.

— Chef Rodrigo, Founder
